小亚洲文化节

作者：约克学联 2017-05-30
  • 学联活动

本文转载自约克学联微信公共帐号

Hi, everyone, welcome to the Little Asian Culture Event!

Gamelan Music Workshop
Date: 12th June 2017
Venue: Music Department

Chinese Tea Ceremony
Date: 13th June 2017, 1pm-5pm
Venue: Library Lawn

Indonesia Cooking Session
Date: 14th June 2017, 1pm-5pm
Venue: Warren

Don’t get it wrong, it’s not about short little guys in Asia. hahaha. Just a funny name for fun.

YOU will get a chance to have a taste of:

  • how to play Gamelan;
  • how to make Chinese tea art;
  • and how to cook Indonesia Yellow Chicken Soup.

Let’s do it together, to learn, to discuss and to practice.

If you are interested in Asian culture, it is a good chance to learn.
The most important thing, let’s make new friends through the event.

All the staffs, students and families are welcome to join us!

Special thanks to GSA community fund supporting this event!

For more details, please contact Zhuoni Li (Lily) email: zl865@york.ac.uk
