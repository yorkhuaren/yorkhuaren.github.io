利兹华人协会2017联欢会

作者：约克华人网 2017-01-22
  • 2017鸡年庆典
  • 利兹华人协会
  • 活动

利兹华人协会2017鸡年庆典将于2017年１月29日在Leeds Town Hall。约克的小伙伴们也可以去参加喔。坐火车到利兹火车站后步行10分钟就到了。以下是利兹华人协会网站的说明：

The Leeds Chinese Community Association is once again hosting a Chinese New Year Festival for the 2017 arrival of the Year of the Rooster . This year event takes place on Sunday 29th January 2017 at Leeds Town Hall. The occasion promises to deliver a much wider Chinese experience than at previous events, as in addition to the usual theatre performances of Dancing, Music, Chinese Lion Dance, Chinese Calligraphy, Kung Fu and Tai Chi, there will also be trade stands offering a variety of Chinese Food, Crafts, Beauty and Face Painting. Tickets on sale from 10.30 am and the workshops begin 11.00am. Many thanks in advance to all those who have already shown support for the event and we look forward to seeing them and everyone else on the day.
